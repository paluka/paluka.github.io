Research Interests & Focus of Work
My research interests lie within the realm of human-computer interaction (HCI). Specifically
  • Natural user interfaces (NUI)
  • Spatial and multi-touch interaction
  • Information visualization
  • Collaborative learning and computer-supported cooperative work
  • User experience
  • User interface design
My work focuses on designing and developing interactive software systems by employing user-centered design processes and evaluating them by designing and conducting research studies with human participants.
